Why do I need a website for my business?

Even local small businesses that operate only in their city need a website. The website is the first thing customers visit when they are looking for a product or service. Having a website will help your business to:

  • Promote and sell its products and services
  • Connect with new customers (and retain current ones)
  • Establish credibility
  • Compete with larger companies
  • Control your brand and keep marketing up-to-date

And best of all: websites are much cheaper and more affordable than you think. GoDaddy offers a full range of online tools for creating websites, along with hosting, email, and marketing options to help your business grow on the Internet.

Why do I need a business email?

There are many reasons to switch from free email to one with your own domain, but these are the top 3:

  1. Customers are more likely to trust addresses like yourname@yourcompany.com than yourcompany_madrid84@notmail.com.
  2. Professional email provides first-class security with industry-leading antivirus and antispam filters.
  3. Custom email addresses promote your business more effectively. Every time you send an email, you're indicating your web address to your customers and potential clients, making it easier for them to visit.
